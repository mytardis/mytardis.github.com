Overview

The main website for the MyTardis project is mytardis.org.

Here and on the wiki you will find community resources, such as a list of apps and filters, as well as MyTardis enhancement proposals and other project information.

MyTardis Enhancement Proposals

Proposals are submitted, dicussed and accepted as pull requests against files in the _posts directory in https://github.com/mytardis/mytardis.github.io.
